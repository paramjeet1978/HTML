Recipe: Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Take two bread slices
  2. Apply butter on bread
  3. Add sliced vegetables
  4. Sprinkle salt
  5. Close the sandwich and serve
